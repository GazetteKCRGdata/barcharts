Percentage of black students suspended at Cedar Rapids schools

At Cedar Rapids schools, black students account for 18.6 percent of district wide enrollment, but 46.5 percent of all suspensions are handed down to black students. These charts break those figures down by school.


Note: "Black enrollment" shows the percentage of black students at the school. "Suspension rate" shows the percentage of all suspensions that were handed down to black students.


Note: Polk AEC was omitted from this list because enrollment data was not available.



Source: Iowa Department of Education | Cedar Rapids School District
