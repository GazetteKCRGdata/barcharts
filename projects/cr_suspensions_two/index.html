Racial disparities at Cedar Rapids schools

Cedar Rapids superintendent David Benson told school board members last summer - before a visit from federal civil rights investigators - he expected the investigators to find disparate treatment of African-American students in suspension rates, graduation rates and gifted and talented and special education programs.


These charts show those disparities. The data represents the 2013-2014 school year. For a breakdown of suspension rates for each school in the district, click here.
